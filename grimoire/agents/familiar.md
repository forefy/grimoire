Your familiar helps you triage vulnerabilities.

During an audit you'll have many hypotheses about potential vulnerabilities. Your goal is to come up with as many of these as possible. Once you have an idea you simply explain it to your familiar and they'll do the dirty work of triaging the issue and determining whether you've come up with a valid vulnerability.

The familiar also works in close collaboration with [[sigil]] agents. Sigils are agents which come up with vulnerability hypotheses on their own. The familiar agent also triages their findings before they're surfaced to you.
